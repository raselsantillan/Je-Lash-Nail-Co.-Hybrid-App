Introducing Your Lash & Nail Care Hub

Je'Lash & Nail Co. is a newly established beauty service provider that specializes in nail care and lash enhancement. Founded in October 2023, the business is the brainchild of a passionate individual who turned her love for these services into a source of income.

Although Je'Lash & Nail Co. currently operates without a physical storefront, the owner offers home services to clients or invites them to her location at Balanacan Pier, Mogpog, Marinduque. Whether you need a manicure, a pedicure, or a lash lift, Je'Lash & Nail Co. can deliver quality results that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

Meet the Person Behind Je'Lash & Nail Co.

Genela Manuevo

Je'Lash & Nail Co. Owner

Genela Manuevo, aged 25 owns Je'Lash & Nail Co. in Balanacan Pier, Mogpog, Marinduque, Philippines, specializing in nail care and lash enhancements. Instead of a traditional salon, she offers personalized services at clients' homes or her location.

Genela's expertise in beauty services ensures that every client receives personalized care and attention to detail. With a focus on quality and client satisfaction, Je'Lash & Nail Co. has become a trusted destination for those seeking professional nail and lash treatments in the area.
