Meet the Person Behind Je'Lash & Nail Co.

Genela Manuevo

Je'Lash & Nail Co. Owner

Genela Manuevo, aged 25 owns Je'Lash & Nail Co. in Balanacan Pier, Mogpog, Marinduque, Philippines, specializing in nail care and lash enhancements. Instead of a traditional salon, she offers personalized services at clients' homes or her location.

Genela's expertise in beauty services ensures that every client receives personalized care and attention to detail. With a focus on quality and client satisfaction, Je'Lash & Nail Co. has become a trusted destination for those seeking professional nail and lash treatments in the area.

What sets Genela apart is not just her skill but also her warm hospitality and commitment to creating a welcoming environment for her clients. At Je'Lash & Nail Co., customers can expect a relaxing and rejuvenating experience that leaves them feeling pampered and beautiful.

For anyone looking for top-notch nail care and lash enhancements, Genela Manuevo's Je'Lash & Nail Co. offers a perfect blend of expertise, personalized service, and a friendly atmosphere, ensuring a delightful beauty experience every time.
